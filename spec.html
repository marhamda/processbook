Preparing the Deliverables

The Design Specification

Our Design Specification identifies our problem statement, our scope, design audience and describes every rational behind our major design choices.In our initial draft, I wrote the problem statement, Kyle wrote the scope and Jennifer wrote the design rational.

The Video

We discussed the storyboard for the video in class. We wanted to give our viewers a clear idea of what our problem space is. Our main character(Kyle), prepares himself to write a paper, but immediately becomes distracted by other things on his computer. A friend tells him about StudySesh and he is finally able to focus.

Putting it all Together

We all met one evening to film the introduction. Kyle offered to work on the screencast portion of the video and Jennifer edited the overall film. While Kyle and Jennifer worked on the video, I worked on putting the specification together. We wrote the first draft before making major changes to our design so there was a lot of change to be made in our final draft. By the Sunday night before finals week, both the video and specification were done.
