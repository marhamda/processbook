Critiquing our Design

After building the initial prototype, our design still had a long way to go. We performed several critiques in order to refine the design into something better

Once again, our group conducted interviews and user tests. This round of interviews provided us with a lot of helpful feedback for improving the design. Our test users found some features confusing and not as intuitive as we thought. They also provided suggestions for features to add to the design.



As a group, we critiqued the visual design of our prototype. We made a list of changes to make in order to improve the designs aesthetics. We looked at the typography, layout, wording and diagrams.
