The Design Process of

Mariam Davis

Group Members: Kyle Bodovitz and Jennifer Luu

Info 360 2012: Team K

This process book explains the story of our design. The task was to address an information problem most students face in the college environment and come up with a design that solves this problem. As students of the University of Washington, my group members and I had no problem listing the challenges college students face. We decided that many students have an issue focusing on schoolwork and because this can impact grades, this is a major problem.

Our group tested various ideas and conducted research to find the main source of this problem. Designing our solution involved interviews, sketches, user testing and evaluations. It also involved a good amount of creativity and team collaboration. In the end, our group came out with a design for a program that blocks computer distractions enabling students to focus better on schoolwork while also collecting data relevant to the distractions and presenting this information to the user at the end of the session. This book will go over the details that led to our final design including how our design evolved throughout the process.
